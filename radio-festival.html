What it does

Festival functions

This page is a vertical profile. The Festival is a sector-building instrument that complements programme execution and research.

Convening

Brings community media actors together for agenda-setting and collaboration.

Practice exchange

Showcases methods, formats, toolkits, and approaches that stations can adopt.

Visibility

Creates institutional visibility for a sector that is often overlooked.

Partnership bridge

Creates contact surfaces between funders, industry, academia, and practitioners.

Standards

Supports the spread of SOPs and shared reporting norms across the ecosystem.

Future directions

Hosts future-facing conversations on tech, content, trust, and sector resilience.
