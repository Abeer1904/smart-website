SDG Toolkit

The SDG Toolkit is designed to help community media organisations align programming with Sustainable Development Goals while retaining hyperlocal relevance.

Executive Summary

This toolkit translates SDG frameworks into implementable broadcast and narrowcast strategies for community radio. It provides SOPs, sample scripts, monitoring templates, and engagement structures that allow stations to produce measurable development outcomes.

Key Sections

  • Understanding SDGs in local governance contexts
  • Mapping SDGs to radio formats
  • Community engagement models
  • Monitoring and reporting templates
