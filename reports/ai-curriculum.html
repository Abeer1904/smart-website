AI Curriculum

The AI Curriculum provides a structured approach to building AI literacy within community media networks and rural audiences.

Executive Summary

This curriculum demystifies artificial intelligence for hyperlocal contexts. It includes broadcast-ready modules, facilitator guides, ethical frameworks, and case-based discussions on AI adoption, misinformation, and rural digital transitions.

Core Modules

  • What is AI? Localised explanations
  • AI and misinformation
  • AI tools for community media
  • Ethics, bias, and accountability
