Partner value

Civic infrastructure: what the ecosystem enables

Community media is critical civic infrastructure. SMART’s enables access to communities.

Reach and trust

Community media provides direct access to local audiences through trusted voices. RJs are embedded local influencers with sustained relationships, which improves reach, credibility, and message retention.

Engagement and insight

The network enables more than dissemination. Broadcast combined with narrowcast formats creates structured feedback loops that help test ideas, surface barriers, and strengthen adoption.

Scale with governance

SMART coordinates distributed implementation through playbooks, SOPs, reporting templates, and central aggregation. This enables a proven method of scale, with measurable outputs and outcomes.

Long-term ecosystem strength

Strengthening community media over time

Each programme strengthens the ecosystem by expanding institutional capacity, improving implementation standards, and producing publishable learning that can be reused across themes and geographies.
